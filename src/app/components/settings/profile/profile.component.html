About Parking

Godowlia Multi Level Two-Wheeler Parking is a dedicated two-wheeler parking located at Godowlia Crossing, On the way to Shree Kashi Vishwanath Temple, Varanasi. It has a capacity of 375 two-wheelers and has a G+4 steel structure with Police booth and Pumping Station on the ground floor.
